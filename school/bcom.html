About My BCom

My journey through the Bachelor of Commerce began on the strong foundation of my VCE success in subjects like Economics and Accounting. I’ve always had a deep interest in understanding how the financial world works, and I carried that passion into my university studies. While I focused heavily on academics and didn’t explore many extracurriculars like clubs, case comps, or internships, I’m still grateful for the solid theoretical grounding I gained in commerce, finance, and economics — knowledge that continues to shape how I see the world.
