About My M-IT

I began my Master of Information Technology with minimal coding experience, having completed only one programming subject. I take comfort in knowing that computer science, software engineering, and IT are om vogue with abundant online resources for self-learning. I chose the Master of Information Technology over its sibling, the Master of Software Engineering, to focus on technical subjects and avoid project management subjects that are poorly run at a university's level.
