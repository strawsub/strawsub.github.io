About My VCE

I moved to Australia in Year 9 as an international student. McKinnon Secondary College was a relatively competitive non-selective government school and studied the entire six subjects allocated.

A quick heads-up: Most of the answers and explanations here have been written by me, but occasionally you might find material that isn’t original. If you come across any potential copyright issues, please don’t hesitate to contact me. Also, not everything on this site is guaranteed to be correct — if it were, I’d have scored a perfect raw 50 in all four subjects! One more thing: the content is based on the 2020 coronavirus-era study design, which means some subjects might be missing one or two areas of study compared to current outlines.
