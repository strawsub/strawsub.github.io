Master of Information Technology

The University of Melbourne

Feb 2023 - Dec 2025

The Master of Information Technology at The University of Melbourne was a natural choice for its:

  • Ease of entry: The only prerequisites were my single coding subject and a Bachelor degree.
  • Breadth of specialisations: I wasn’t certain on the area I would like to focus on. Artificial intelligence was the trend, but cyber security was the security—yet all I wanted was to write software.

My initial choice of specialisation was the vanilla Computing. However, due to the lack of interesting electives, I eventually relocated to Artificial Intelligence. Both share more similarities than differences in terms of their course structure.

Bachelor of Commerce

The University of Melbourne

Feb 2021 - Dec 2023

I chose Commerce because I performed well in Commerce subjects during high school. I didn't have a strong passion for anything at the time. The University of Melbourne provided me with the most generous scholarship, and was the highest ranked university in Melbourne.

I majored in Accounting & Finance. Accounting promised the best job security. Finance was quite straightforward for a black-and-white person like myself.
